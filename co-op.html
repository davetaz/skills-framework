Data literacy framework

The Co-operative

We live in the age of data. Data is the new raw material that is building new businesses, driving innovation and efficiency. From the supply chain, to human resources, there are a huge range of opportunities for the Co-op. At the same time we must ensure that data is collected, used and shared in ways that ensures we maintain our values, particularly around the legal and ethical handling of such data.

We are working with the Open Data Institute who share many of our values, to build a data literacy framework and skills programme to help those in our community get the most from data whilst protecting people from harmful impacts.
